Title:Moncton Wildcats shut out Cape Breton Eagles 30
Content:AVENIR CENTRE MONCTON Olivier Rodrigue made 28 saves leading the Moncton Wildcats to a 30 victory over the Cape Breton Eagles in QMJHL action.
Gabriel Fortier added a goal and an assist for the Wildcats. Kevin Mandolese led the Eagles with 44 saves.
